# 🛠️ Narrative Arc Weaver

> *The Narrative Arc Weaver*

---

**The problem:** Untangle academic jargon and weave compelling audio narratives from complex research.

**What it does:** A tool that takes complex research text and generates a structured, engaging narrative outline optimized for audio presentation, focusing on storytelling elements, audience hooks, and jargon-free explanations.

**Born from:** ⚠️ Reddit | QUOTE: "The hardest part is taking dense academic papers and turning them into something conversational and engaging. It's not just summarizing; it's finding the story, the flow, the hooks. My listeners don't want a lecture; they want to understand *why* it matters, without getting lost in jargon."
