# 🛠️ Echo Bloom

> *Echo Bloom: Your Gentle Digital Rhythm Weaver*

---

**The problem:** This tool helps you embrace your body's unique rhythm, transforming digital demands into a gentle, affirming ambient aesthetic for your day.

**What it does:** Echo Bloom takes a plain text description of your current energy or mood and generates a poetic ambient descriptor, a subtle color palette, and an abstract SVG pattern to softly reflect your internal state in your digital space.
