# 🛠️ Name Fold Animator

> *The Unfolding Name Jar*

---

**The problem:** A tool that doesn’t fix your name — it holds its unfolding.

**What it does:** A single HTML page that lets someone write two versions of their name (e.g., “Sarah Chen” / “Sarah Kim”, “Jamie Lopez” / “Jamie Lopez-Rivera”, “they/them” / “she/her”) and gently animate their coexistence — not as a toggle, but as overlapping layers that breathe, shift weight, and settle at their own pace.

**Born from:** ⚠️ r/divorce | QUOTE: "I signed my divorce papers last week and kept signing my old name on everything — coffee order, pharmacy form, even my own journal. It’s not that I *forgot*. It’s like my hand knows a version of me that doesn’t exist anymore, and it won’t stop writing her."  
r/GriefSupport | QUOTE: "After Mom died, I caught myself answering ‘How are you?’ with ‘We’re doing okay’ — using the plural, like she was still in the room. It’s been eight months. My mouth hasn’t caught up to my life."
