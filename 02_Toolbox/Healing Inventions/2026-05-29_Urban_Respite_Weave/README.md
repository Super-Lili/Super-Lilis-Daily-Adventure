# 🛠️ Urban Respite Weave

> *Urban Respite Weave: Your Digital Oasis in the City's Hum*

---

**The problem:** Offers a digital calm-down kit for urban sensory overload, transforming your screen into a quiet, ambient escape.

**What it does:** This tool generates an interactive HTML app that provides a minimalist visual and ambient soundscape to help users find a moment of calm amidst urban sensory overload.

**Born from:** ⚠️ `https://reflections.live/articles/psychological-toll-of-sensory-overload-in-cities`
