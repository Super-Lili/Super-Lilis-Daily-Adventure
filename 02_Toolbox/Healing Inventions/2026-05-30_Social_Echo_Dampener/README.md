# 🛠️ Social Echo Dampener

> *The Quiet Afterglow: Softening Social Static*

---

**The problem:** Ever feel those social echoes long after the conversation ends? This gentle tool helps you find your quiet after the "on" button finally switches off.

**What it does:** This tool provides a minimalist, interactive HTML app that guides users through a series of gentle prompts and subtle sensory shifts to help process and release residual mental "social static" after demanding interactions.
