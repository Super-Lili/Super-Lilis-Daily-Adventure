# 🛠️ Presence Weaver

> *The Presence Weaver: Reclaiming Your Digital Soul*

---

**The problem:** Ever feel like your attention is a scattered handful of glitter? This tool helps you gather it back, one mindful digital moment at a time!

**What it does:** This tool guides you to set an intention before engaging in digital activities and prompts you to reflect afterward, helping you cultivate presence and intentionality in your digital life.

**Born from:** ⚠️ [https://www.reddit.com/r/offmychest/comments/1cn104q/i_think_social_media_slowly_changed_me_emotionally_and_i_didnt_notice_it_happening/](https://www.reddit.com/r/offmychest/comments/1cn104q/i_think_social_media_slowly_changed_me_emotionally_and_i_didnt_notice_it_happening/)
