# 🛠️ Sensory Baseline Weaver

> *The Sensory Baseline Weaver*

---

**The problem:** Gently guides your senses back to calm, one subtle shift at a time, after a day of digital demands.

**What it does:** A tool that creates a dynamic HTML environment to progressively reduce visual and auditory stimuli, helping creative professionals reset their nervous system after intense digital exposure.

**Born from:** ⚠️ NDTV | QUOTE: "After a long workday, amid constant sensory stimulation, the nervous system can become overloaded. This can result in brain fatigue, anxiety, and feeling wired but tired." "Introducing silence or white noise can reduce this overload, and be sure to avoid screens and notification pings that make the brain feel like it has to pay attention to something all the time."
