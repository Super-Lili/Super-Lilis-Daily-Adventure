# 🛠️ Brand Voice Aligner

> *The Tone Whisperer*

---

**The problem:** Helps creative teams keep their brand voice on point, making sure every word sings with the right tone, no matter who's writing.

**What it does:** A tool that analyzes provided text against defined brand style guide elements (keywords, tone descriptors, prohibited terms) and provides a consistency score and specific suggestions for improving brand voice alignment.

**Born from:** ⚠️ Threads and X posts by creative studio workers
