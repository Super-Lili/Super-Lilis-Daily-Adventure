# 🛠️ Rhythm Weaver

> *The Rhythmic Eye*

---

**The problem:** Uncover the hidden beats and subtle missteps in your typography with a visual guide to perfect rhythm.

**What it does:** A tool that analyzes text and font properties, then generates a visual heatmap and specific recommendations to improve typographic rhythm, optical balance, and line breaks.

**Born from:** ⚠️ r/typography | QUOTE: "Achieving a harmonious vertical rhythm across different text elements on a page is a beast. My current process involves a lot of manual grid overlays and pixel nudging. Would love something that could analyze the spacing and tell me where the rhythm breaks."
