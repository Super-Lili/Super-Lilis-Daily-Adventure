# 🛠️ FocusFlow Guardian

> *The FocusFlow Guardian: Your Sanctuary in the Meeting Storm*

---

**The problem:** Reclaim your precious deep work hours from the clutches of endless meetings and calendar chaos.

**What it does:** This tool analyzes your meeting schedule from a CSV file and proposes dedicated focus blocks to help you balance collaborative time with essential deep work, exporting the suggested schedule to a new CSV file.

**Born from:** ⚠️ [https://slowchathealth.com/2026/05/07/rethinking-productivity-for-school-practitioners/](https://slowchathealth.com/2026/05/07/rethinking-productivity-for-school-practitioners/)

---
