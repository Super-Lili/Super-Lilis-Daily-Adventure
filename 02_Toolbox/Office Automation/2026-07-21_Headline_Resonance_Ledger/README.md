# 🛠️ Headline Resonance Ledger

> *The Headline Hoarder’s Ledger*

---

**The problem:** What if your abandoned headlines weren’t trash—but terrain?

**What it does:** A tool that imports your past headline drafts (plain text or CSV), tags them by campaign, audience mood, and send date, then renders them as a navigable, chronologically anchored visual ledger—with no scoring, only contextual resonance.

**Born from:** ⚠️ r/writing | QUOTE: “I draft 17 headlines for one newsletter. Save them all in a Notes app. Then I scroll past them for three days, paralyzed—not because none are good, but because I can’t tell which one *lands* without sending it to real people. And A/B testing feels like cheating on my own voice.”  
r/Newsletters | QUOTE: “My ‘headline graveyard’ folder has 212 entries. I reuse old ones when I’m tired, and clients always say ‘this feels familiar’—but I don’t know *which* version they remember, or why.”
