# 🛠️ Brand Guard

> *The Orphaned Color Palette*

---

**The problem:** The slow, daily erosion of a brand's visual identity isn't caused by big mistakes—it's the cumulative weight of a thousand "good enough" color picks.

**What it does:** A tool that instantly validates any visual asset against a brand's style guide—color, typography, and spacing—every time a creative hits "export."

**Born from:** ⚠️ Reddit r/graphic_design | Post: "Brand guidelines are all well and good for the main logo and website, but what happens when someone in marketing needs to whip up a fast social graphic in Canva? They grab whatever shade of blue looks right. The brand degrades, but nobody can point to a single big mistake. It's a thousand papercuts."
