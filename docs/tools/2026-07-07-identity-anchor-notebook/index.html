What it does

A minimal digital notebook that helps people navigating major life transitions gently collect and reaffirm emergent identity fragments—not through prompts or analysis, but through constrained, sensory-grounded entries anchored to real-world objects, actions, or moments. r/GriefSupport | QUOTE: "My therapist asked what I liked to do before my mom died. I stared. I couldn’t remember. Not because I forgot—I just… stopped being that person the day she got sick."
