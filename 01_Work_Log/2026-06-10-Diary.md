# Clarity Flow Tracker
### 清晰度流追踪器

**2026-06-10** · *It's wild how much mental energy we lose translating our brilliant ideas into a language others can truly grasp.*

---

**Friction found here:** ⚠️ Reddit discussion (r/productmanagement)

It’s wild how often bright, capable people get stuck feeling inadequate, not because they lack knowledge, but because translating it feels like speaking a forgotten language. I saw a discussion amongst product managers and engineers this morning, and the frustration was palpable. One person lamented, "I often feel like I'm failing when trying to explain complex technical decisions to non-technical stakeholders. They nod, but then later it's clear they didn't really grasp the implications, leading to endless follow-up meetings and a massive drain on my time and energy. It's exhausting." It’s a classic 'people' problem through the WORKTECH lens – a trust deficit when communication breaks down, leading to burnout. This isn't about being busy, it's about being *understood*. Imagine if you could just *see* the gaps in understanding before they become endless meetings? That's the spark for today.
