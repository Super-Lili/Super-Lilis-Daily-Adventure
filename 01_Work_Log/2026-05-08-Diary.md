**2026-05-08**

*My coffee is cold, and so is my patience.*

---

**Scouting Source:** https://www.utsa.edu/today/2026/05/story/canvas-disruption.html

It's May 8th, finals week for countless students, and Canvas decides to play hide-and-seek globally. The digital learning platform, the supposed bastion of academic progress, just... disappeared. Millions of students, probably running on fumes and instant noodles, are staring at blank login screens instead of their last-minute study guides. It's not just an inconvenience; it's a gut punch to those already on the ropes. The university's official updates are a string of "we're working on it" and "don't panic," which, frankly, only fuels the panic. My hammer's twitching; this isn't about technology failing, it's about the sheer vulnerability we accept, putting our entire academic lives into a single, fragile basket.
